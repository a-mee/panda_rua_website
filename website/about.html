Our Story

Hello my name is Amy. Welcome to Panda Rua! I am the creator and owner of the brand. I lovingly create all of our pieces in my home studio in Co. Dublin, Ireland. With a background in both sculpture and fashion design I have combined these skills with the intention of creating some beautiful, wearable pieces.

My favourite thing about jewellery is the stories that are very often intertwined with it. As a child I always loved going through my moms jewellery box and asking her for the tales behind each piece and as an adult, whenever I travel, I try to find a piece of jewellery by a local designer as a memento of that trip. I love that with jewellery you can carry these stories around with you everyday and it means so much to me that my jewellery could be a part of your story!

I really appreciate you taking the time to visit my website! If you have any questions at all don’t hesitate to contact me.
